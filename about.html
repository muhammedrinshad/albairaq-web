Who We Are

Al Bairaq Oil Field Services and Maintenance is a prominent service provider supplying leading edge services principally to the oil and gas sectors.

Al Bairaq is a 100% Emirati owned company, focusing on onshore and offshore oil and gas industries established in the year of 2008.Our business is generally concentrated in Middle East on the public work sector, power and water industries, marine industry, and defense sector. Our company is owned and managed by a team of highly performing experienced professionals so the clients will, as always; receive broad range of integrated services with an excellent focus on HSE.

Our Partners and Supporters

System Kikou Co.,Ltd.

System Kikou Co., Ltd (SKK) is a pionior and leading company of oil sludge treatment with 300 commercial experiences in the last 10 years. The application of our technology brings huge economical benefit by oil recovery from waste sludge together with environmental protection. SKK has expanded its service and business in maintenance and ecology fields through continuous efforts of technology development.
